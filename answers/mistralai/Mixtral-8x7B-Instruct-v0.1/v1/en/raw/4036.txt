## Answer: In case of a medical emergency, you can find help through the medical treatment facilities available in Augsburg. You can use the online doctor and hospital search tools provided in the links to find a suitable healthcare provider. If you need advice on health or the health system in Germany, you can contact the Department of Health (Staatliches Gesundheitsamt). For special social services, you can contact the Special Social Services (BSD) for adults. If you are pregnant or have a newborn child, you can find counseling services in the section "Family, Children, Young People and Senior Citizens" under "Counseling for pregnant women and new parents". ## Numbers: 1, 3, 4, 6, 14, 16, 19, 22, 28 ##
