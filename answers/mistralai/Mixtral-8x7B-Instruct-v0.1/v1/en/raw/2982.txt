## Answer: The integration course consists of a language course and an orientation course. It is for foreigners with a permanent residence permit, asylum seekers with a residence permit, foreigners with a tolerated stay permit, EU citizens, and German nationals who do not yet speak sufficient German. The language course can be 600 teaching units and can be free of charge for refugees, asylum seekers, and other people in the target group who receive social benefits. The course can be full-time or part-time and can take place in local advice centers or integration courses.
