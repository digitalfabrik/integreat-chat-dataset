## Answer: The adult education center (VHS) Augsburger Land e.V. offers numerous courses and events for older people. You can find the current program at www.vhs-augsburger-land.de, in the town halls of the municipalities and at the Augsburg District Office. Other facilities such as clubs or churches also have a lot to offer.
