## Answer: To vote in Germany's elections, you need to cast 40 votes for the candidates of the Migration Advisory Board. You can do this by either splitting your votes among candidates on different lists, giving extra support to individual candidates through cumulating, voting for a complete list, or voting for a list with changes. You can also use an online test ballot paper to practice casting your votes. ## Numbers: 3, 14-25, 27-32, 34-36, 38-44, 46-48
