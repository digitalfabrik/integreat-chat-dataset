## Answer: Your child is allowed to go to school. After 6 months in Germany at the latest, you must register your child at a school. School attendance is compulsory. Ask at the town hall which school your child can go to. There are special classes to learn German. Please take your child to the school to register. You will need the following documents: Your identity card, birth certificate or proof of arrival, Registration certificate, all documents relating to your child (ID/passport, birth certificate, school reports, medical certificates,...). ## Numbers: 2,3,4,5,6,12,13,14,15 ##
