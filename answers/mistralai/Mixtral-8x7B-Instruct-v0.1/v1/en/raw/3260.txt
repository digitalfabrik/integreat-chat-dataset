## Answer: The district of Augsburg provides a contraceptive fund that covers the cost of contraceptives for certain people. To apply, you must meet the requirements of residence in the district, being at least 23 years old, and receiving social benefits. The costs covered include the pill, copper IUD, hormone coil, hormone implant, three-month injection, vaginal ring, contraceptive patch, and sterilization for women and men. You can submit your application to the pregnancy counseling service at the Augsburg District Office along with the required documents. ## Numbers: 2, 4, 11, 12, 13-18, 20-29, 31-32, 34, 37, 39-43
