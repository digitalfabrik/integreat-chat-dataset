## Answer: A disabled person's pass can be applied for at the Bavarian Center for Families and Social Affairs. It entitles the holder to certain benefits. Information on financial aid for people with disabilities can be found on the website of the Federal Ministry of Labor and Social Affairs (www.bmas.de). The guide "My child is disabled - what help is available" provides a good overview of rights and financial assistance for people with disabilities and their families. It is also available in Turkish, Russian, Arabic, and Vietnamese. ## Numbers: 1, 4, 5, 11, 13, 14, 15 ##
