## Answer: Public transport in Munich is called ÖPNV (öffentlicher Personennahverkehr). There are several different maps available for S-Bahn, U-Bahn, regional train, tram, and bus. A detailed timetable can be found on the MVG website. The MVG app can help with navigation. ## Numbers: 3, 8-10, 12-16, 19, 20, 22, 24, 26, 28, 30
