## Answer: To cancel your contract with the license fee (GEZ), you need to check if you have a special case for termination and adhere to the minimum contract term with your old provider. Some people may be exempt from this fee obligation, for example if you receive social benefits or have other reasons for exemption. More information is available at www.rundfunkbeitrag.de. If you are an asylum seeker, you have no legal right to television in your asylum accommodation and should show any invoice for the broadcasting fee to your accommodation provider. ## Numbers: 3, 4, 8, 9, 10, 12, 15, 16
