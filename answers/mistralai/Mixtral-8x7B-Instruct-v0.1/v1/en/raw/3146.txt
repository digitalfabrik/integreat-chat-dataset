## Answer: Technical colleges offer in-depth vocational training for people who have completed vocational training or have professional experience. The duration of the course can be up to two years full-time or up to 4 years part-time. The degrees offered include a state final examination or master craftsman examination, secondary school leaving certificate, general higher education entrance qualification, or Fachhochschulreife (after a supplementary examination). ## Numbers: 5, 6, 3, 4, 11, 12, 14, 15, 17
