## Answer: An open meeting place or an open café is a place where people meet. It doesn't matter whether they have an appointment or simply come to this place spontaneously. This offers the opportunity to get in touch with a wide variety of people and exchange ideas. There are also open cafés in many communities in the district with different names like intercultural meeting café, café international, women's café. Ask at your local town hall for an open meeting place or café. ## Numbers: 1,2,3,4,5,6
