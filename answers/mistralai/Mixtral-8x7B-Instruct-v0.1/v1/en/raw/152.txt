## Answer: A settlement permit can be issued if you have legal residence in Germany for at least five years, have a valid residence permit that was not issued for a temporary purpose or for humanitarian reasons, can prove a secure livelihood and living space, have proof of sufficient pension provision, and can demonstrate sufficient German language skills and basic knowledge of the legal and social system in Germany. ## Numbers: 5,6,7,8,10,12

## Answer: The EU permanent residence permit offers the same rights as the settlement permit, and allows you to live and work in Germany without any time or place restrictions.
