Additional information:
- Officially recognized documents in Germany can only be translated by state-certified translators.
- Examples of such documents include diplomas, marriage certificates, and birth certificates.
- These translations can be expensive.
- It is recommended to verify beforehand if a certified translation is truly necessary.
