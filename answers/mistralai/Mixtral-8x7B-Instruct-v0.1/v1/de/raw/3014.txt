## Answer: In Germany, you cannot work more than 8 hours a day, but in exceptional cases, the workday can be extended to 10 hours if the average daily working time over a period of 6 months does not exceed 8 hours. Also, you are entitled to breaks: at least 30 minutes after 6 hours of work and at least 45 minutes after 9 hours of work. ## Numbers: 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7 ##
