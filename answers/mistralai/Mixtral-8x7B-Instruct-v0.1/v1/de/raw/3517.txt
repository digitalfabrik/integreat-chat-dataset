## Answer: You can find courses for older people at the Volkshochschule (VHS) Augsburger Land e.V. with 34 different locations in the district. Other options include local community centers, churches, and senior clubs. Information about these courses can be found on the VHS website, in local town halls, and on the Landkreis Augsburg website. ## Numbers: 2, 5, 9, 10, 11
