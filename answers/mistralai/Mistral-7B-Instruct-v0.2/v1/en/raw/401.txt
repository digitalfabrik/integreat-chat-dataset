## Answer: For citizens who are at least 22 years old and receive subsistence assistance or basic income support (SGB XII), citizen's allowance or social benefit (SGB II), benefits under the Asylum Seekers Benefits Act (AsylbLG), housing benefit or child supplement, or are people on low incomes (except au pairs, trainees and students), the City of Munich covers the costs of medically prescribed contraceptives.
