## Answer: Citizens of EU countries, EEA countries, and persons with Swiss nationality do not require a work permit to work in Germany. Persons with a residence permit also have access to the labor market. Refugees with a residence permit are allowed to work. Asylum seekers in ongoing proceedings can apply for a work permit after nine months or three months, depending on their country of origin and asylum application status. Tolerated persons can apply for a work permit after six months of toleration or three months, depending on their country of origin and toleration status.
