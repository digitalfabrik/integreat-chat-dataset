## Answer: Tolerated persons who have already started training during the asylum procedure or have been in possession of a tolerated stay permit for three months are eligible for the tolerated stay permit to train. The application must include a training contract, be submitted via the Foreigners' Registration Office, and meet the following requirements: qualified training in a state-recognized or comparably regulated training occupation with a standard training period of at least two years, entry of the vocational training in the register of vocational training relationships, and a clarified identity.
