## Answer: The German Pension Insurance (DRV) is a social security system in Germany that provides pensions and other benefits for people who can no longer work in old age. It is funded by contributions from employers and employees. The pension is not paid automatically and must be applied for at least 3 months before retirement. The German Pension Insurance also offers benefits such as rehabilitation measures to help people get back to work and maintain or even increase their pension benefits.
