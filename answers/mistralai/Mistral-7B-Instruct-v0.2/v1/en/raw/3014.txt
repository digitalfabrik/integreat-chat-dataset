## Answer: The working hours in Germany cannot exceed 8 hours a day, with exceptions for up to 10 hours in exceptional cases where the average of 8 hours per day has not been exceeded within 6 months. Employees are also entitled to breaks after working for 6 hours (30 minutes) and 9 hours (45 minutes).
