At the ANKER Center, people without private accommodation can find a first point of contact and registration takes place on site. The Office for Youth and Family Affairs is also looking for people who take in children and young people without a legal guardian, and they can contact the office for assistance. Power of attorney documents are provided on the website for those who need it.
