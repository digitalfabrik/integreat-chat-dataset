## Numbers: 3 (for EU-Migrants), 9, 11 (for Spätaussiedler)

For EU-Migrants: You need to register at the responsible Resident Registration Office (Einwohnermeldeamt) and have a valid personal ID or passport from your home country.

For Spätaussiedler: After receiving the registration form in your home country, you can enter Germany. You will be assigned to a district by the Federal Office for Administration in Friedland and accommodated if you cannot find private housing. You need a valid passport or ID from your home country.
