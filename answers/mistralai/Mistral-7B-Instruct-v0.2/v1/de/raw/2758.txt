## Answer: Die Mülltrennung bedeutet, dass man den Müll in unterschiedliche Tonnen sortiert und entsorgt, z.B. Plastik, Papier und Pappe in separate Tonnen. ## Numbers: 1, 2, 3

Explanation:
The context mentions that there are different bins or tonnes for different types of waste, such as plastic, paper, and food waste. It also emphasizes the importance of separating waste for recycling and environmental protection. Therefore, the term "Mülltrennung" in this context refers to the practice of separating and sorting waste into different bins or tonnes for proper disposal and recycling.
