## Answer: To apply for a social housing unit in Germany, you need to register an application at the Amt für Wohnen und Migration. You can do this online through the SOWON platform or in paper form.
## Numbers: 3, 4, 8, 9, 14, 15, 16
## Requirements: You must have a permanent residence in Germany, a main residence in Germany, and an income below a certain threshold to be eligible for a social housing unit.
## Numbers: 2
## Documents: You will need to provide income documents, a personal ID, a housing contract or proof of current living situation, and other documents depending on your situation (e.g. proof of pregnancy or guardianship).
## Numbers: 17-24
## Application process: After registering your application, your individual housing need and the urgency of your housing search will be determined. You can then search for housing offers on SOWON that match your household size and income level.
## Numbers: 27-30
## Costs: There are no fees for submitting a housing application, but a fee of 25 Euro is charged for the confirmation of a successful housing placement.
## Numbers: 34, 35
## Contact: For more information, you can visit the Amt für Wohnen und Migration website or contact the customer service center for housing.
