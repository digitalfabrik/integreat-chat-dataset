## Answer: To cancel your GEZ (Radio and Television Contribution) contract, you should contact your current provider and inquire about the cancellation process. Some providers may require a minimum contract term of 24 months. However, there are certain circumstances under which a cancellation may be possible. For more information, visit www.rundfunkbeitrag.de/welcome/index_ger.html.
