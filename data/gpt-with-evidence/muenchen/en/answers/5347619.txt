Q1: Who is eligible for livelihood/health care support?
A1: For people who are in the asylum procedure or have "Duldung" status.
Q2: What does the support include?
A2: Food, accommodation, clothing, health and personal care products, household items, and a monthly amount of money for personal needs.
Q3: How can I make an appointment?
A3: You can make an appointment by contacting the Office for Housing and Migration.
